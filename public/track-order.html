Understanding Order Status

Confirmed

Your order has been received and payment deducted from your salary balance

2

Preparing

3

Ready

4

Picked Up

Order has been collected. Enjoy your meal!

❓ Frequently Asked Questions

How do I refresh my order status?

The page automatically updates every 30 seconds. You can also refresh the page manually to see the latest status.

Can I cancel my order?

Cancel within 2 minutes of placing the order. After that, your meal will be prepared and cannot be cancelled.

What if my order is delayed?

Contact the canteen counter or email us. We'll either expedite your order or offer a refund.

How long can I pick up my order?

Orders must be picked up within 30 minutes of the "Ready" status. After that, items may be redistributed.

Can I modify my order after placing it?

Modifications are possible only within 1 minute of placing the order, subject to availability.

Is there a delivery option?

Currently, all orders are for pickup at the canteen. We're working on delivery options soon!
