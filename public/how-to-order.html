Complete Ordering Process

1

Login to Your Account

Use your email and password. Select your position (Manager, Assistant, Staff, Intern) and department (IT, HR, Finance, Marketing)

2

Check Your Wallet

View your monthly salary allowance (₹150-500 based on position) in your wallet section

3

Browse the Menu

Explore our 12+ dishes across 4 cuisines. Search by name or filter by cuisine type

4

Add Items to Cart

Click "Add to Cart" for items you want. View your cart in the sidebar for quick review

5

Review Your Order

Go to cart, review items and total cost. Check that your balance is sufficient

Place Your Order

Get Confirmation
